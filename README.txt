Company Name: OOP World

Company Description: Founded in 2020 by 3 ambitious Bowdoin Students, OOP World is an upcoming, exciting amusement park! 
OOP World uses classic Computer Science Algorithms such as BFS and Dijkstra to create top of the line rides. 
Our rides also use Machine-Learning techniques to enhance the experience for all users!

Dependencies: Bootstrap, Chrome, Safari
